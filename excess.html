Paragraph On Planting Trees In English [100, 150, 200, 250 ... Trees are vital to life on Earth, providing the oxygen we breathe, absorbing carbon dioxide to clean the air, and regulating the climate by providing shade and managing water cycles. They offer essential food and shelter for wildlife, preventing soil erosion and fostering biodiversity. Trees also support human communities by supplying materials for shelter and medicine and enhancing the beauty of our landscapes. Saving and planting trees are crucial for a healthy environment and a sustainable future for all living things
